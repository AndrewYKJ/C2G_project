Privacy Policy

 

Our Privacy Policy is being provided to you in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Act 709).

 

At Sabah State Government, keeping the personal data of individuals ("data subject") secure is of utmost importance to us. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect this data, which meet or exceed applicable law. This page outlines our practices with regards to the Personal Information we collect through your download and use of the "Citizen Report Feedback System" (the "App") and all contents, products, services and functionalities available through the App.

 

Personal Data

 

What data do we collect from you?

To assist us with your inquiries, establish and operate an account/facility for you and/or provide you with our products and services, we may need to and/or may be required to collect, record, hold, use, disclose, store and process personal information about you, including but not limited to the following:

● personal details such as name, age, gender, date of birth, and race;

● contact details such as district and phone numbers;

● mobile device location;

● images;

● other information relevant to "Citizen Report" Services.

 

Why do we collect your data?

● for identification and authentication purposes;

● for data processing purposes;

● to evaluate and monitor the provision of services;

● to respond to customer inquiries;

● to understand customers' needs and offer products and services to meet those needs;

● for enforcement of our rights and obligations of other parties to us;

● to meet legal and regulatory requirements;

● for risk management;

● for any other purposes as permitted by applicable law or with your consent;

● for all other purposes incidental and associated with any of the above.

 

Third-Party Websites

If you click on a link and are redirected to another site, a third-party entity may collect your personal information. We have no control over these external websites or their content, and we do not review them. As a result, we cannot be responsible for any data collection that occurs after you click on links to connect to these external websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to such external websites or their content.

 

Accessing and Correcting Your Personal Information

Under the PDPA 2010 (Act 709), you have the right to request access to personal data that an organization holds about you. To ensure that we do not disclose your information to anyone who is not authorized to have it, we must verify the identity of anyone making such a request.

If a data subject informs us that the personal data we hold on them is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date, we will make the necessary corrections to our records.

 

Amendments

We reserve the right to amend this policy at any time and from time to time to comply with regulatory requirements.
